About dBA: The dBA scale is a weighted decibel scale that approximates human hearing sensitivity. It is commonly used for environmental and occupational noise measurements.

Common dBA Levels and Effects:

dBA LevelExample / EffectHarmfulness
30 dBAWhisper, quiet librarySafe
50 dBANormal conversationSafe
70 dBABusy traffic, vacuum cleanerSafe for short exposure
85 dBACity traffic, hair dryerHearing damage after prolonged exposure
100 dBAChainsaw, nightclubDangerous after 15 min
120 dBARock concert, thunderclapImmediate harm, pain threshold
140 dBAJet engine at takeoffPain, possible permanent damage

Note: This tool currently measures digital loudness (dBFS), not true dBA SPL. For accurate dBA measurements, a calibrated sound level meter is required.
